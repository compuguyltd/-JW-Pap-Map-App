Foreign Language Slips and Maps

When a map is returned check for foreign language slips (green) and do not call slips.

Look at each DNC slip to see if the publishers have found any mistakes on the slips.


Green slips.
Check each slip has a territory map number.
Go into the map disk and update the house/road number.
Be sure to change the date on the file and reprint the DNC slips.
Throw out old slips and replace with the new.
Open the ethnic group file and update the map/road/number.
Put like languages together and give the green slips to the congregation sectretary.


House to House Records.
Combine where possible onto less sheets. Give to Territory Overseer or groups.
Any dog/locked etc can go on a separate sheet for the telephone witnessing group.
